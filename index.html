    Fall in Love with Our Tandoori Chicken

    Our chicken marinated in aromatic spices and char-grilled to perfection - that's tandoori chicken for you.

    Tandoori chicken is a flavorful dish made by marinating chicken in a blend of yogurt and spices, and cooking it in a tandoor, a traditional clay oven. The high heat of the tandoor creates smoky flavor on the outside of the chicken while keeping the inside tender and juicy. Tandoori chicken is a popular dish in South Asian cuisine, and is often served with a side of mint chutney or raita.
